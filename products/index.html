VBE-018

Vibration Body Exerciser

OVERVIEW

  • Auvus’ Whole Body Vibration Machine Model No VBE-018 is an ergonomically and easy to use exercise machine for personal and professional use at:
    – Home, Clinics, Senior Citizen Centres, Offices
    – Gyms, Spas, Hotels, Airport Lounges
  • The machine is suitable for those:
    – Who want to improve their overall fitness level
    – With limited mobility / chronic health conditions
  • The vibration machine emits soothing and gentle waves of therapeutic vibrations from the platform to exercise and stimulate the whole body. The ergonomically designed handlebar can be adjusted to each user’s personal preference.

FEATURES

  •  Whole Body Vibration (WBV) technology
  •  High performance motor emits tri-planar therapeutic vibrations (3-D vibrations)
  •  Patented motor module produces sinusoidal waves for the body to effectively stimulate muscles, nerves, tendons and bones
  •  Patented spring loaded air suspension system transmits soothing vibration and provides quiet operation
  •  Thick tempered glass vibration platform transmits vibrations uniformly throughout vibration surface
  •  Ergonomic and adjustable handlebar maximizes user comfort
  •  Constructed with durable high tensile steel and composite materials
  •  Compact and light weight
  •  Safe and maintenance-free

SPECIFICATIONS
Vibration Time: 10 minute timer
Vibration Mode: Tri-planar (mostly vertical)
Vibration Type: Sinusoidal waveform
Vibration Frequency: Low / Medium / High; 15 / 25 / 35 Hz (cycles per second)
Maximum Load: 300 lb or 136 Kg
Machine Dimensions: 40 x 60 x 15 cm or 16 x 24 x 6 inches
Machine Weight: 50 Kg or 110 lb
Power Rating: 24Vdc 50W
